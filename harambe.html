Harambe is the name of the gorilla that was shot and killed at the Cincinnati Zoo on May 28, 2016. He was shot because a child had fallen into the pit with him. Zoo workers feared for the kid's life, so they decided to kill Harambe, instead of using a tranquilizer dart. The reason for this, is because tranqulizers take a small amount of time to take effect. If Harambe had felt himself being stuck with something sharp, he could have gotten violent and put the child at risk.

This page is a tribute to Harambe and his greatness. May he rest in peace and in happiness.
